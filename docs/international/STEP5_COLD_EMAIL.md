## 🎯 邮件优化技巧

### 1. 主题行（Subject Line）
**重要性**：决定邮件是否被打开

**好的主题行**：
- ✅ "Quick question about [Project Name]"
- ✅ "DeFi developer for [Project Name]"
- ✅ "Saw you're hiring [role] - can help"
- ✅ "Build your MVP faster"

**坏的主题行**：
- ❌ "Web developer available"（太泛）
- ❌ "Looking for work"（不专业）
- ❌ "Freelance services"（像spam）
- ❌ "Hello"（太模糊）

### 2. 个性化（关键！）
```
❌ 错误（像群发）：
"Hi, I'm a developer. Want to hire me?"

✅ 正确（个性化）：
"Hi [Name],
I saw your tweet about [Project X] - loved the approach!
I noticed you're expanding to Polygon..."
```

### 3. 展示价值（不要只列技能）
```
❌ 错误：
"I know React, Node.js, Solidity"

✅ 正确（展示成果）：
"Built DeFi platform with $500K+ TVL
Saved 30% gas costs vs competitors
Launched in 3 months"
```

### 4. 清晰的行动号召（CTA）
```
✅ 好的CTA：
- "Want to hop on a quick call?"
- "Can I send you my portfolio?"
- "When are you free to chat?"

❌ 模糊的CTA：
- "Let me know if you need help"（被动）
- "Hope to hear from you"（弱）
```
